Behind the build · Process transparency

This portfolio is itself
a case study.

I built this portfolio using AI as a creative collaborator — documenting every prompt, decision, and revision along the way.

It's an experiment in transparency: what does design process look like when you use AI as a tool, not a shortcut? You can see the finished site — and look deeper into how it was built.

View on GitHub ↗
01

Prompt engineering — Translating a design brief into language an AI can interpret, and knowing when to push back.

02

Creative direction — Reviewing, critiquing, and redirecting AI output. AI proposes; I decide.

03

Iteration log — Every version documented on GitHub. You can see exactly what changed and why.

04

Reflection — What worked, what didn't, and what it taught me about using AI as a design tool.
