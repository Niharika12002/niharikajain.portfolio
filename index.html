Open File: Niharika

"I design the way I collect things — noticing patterns, saving details, building stories, and turning scattered pieces into something people can understand."

I'm a UX and graphic designer who graduated from SCAD Atlanta in 2026. My work spans digital product design, visual identity, service design, physical-digital systems, and AI-assisted tools. I think in systems. I notice everything. I care about emotion as much as clarity.

Outside of work: cooking from memory without a recipe, deep inside a murder mystery or a Bollywood film I've already watched twice, haunting every museum within driving distance, photographing skies, being particular about nails, and having a lot of opinions about Indian homegrown brands.

I'm always dressing up for no reason, always collecting things that don't have a category yet, and absolutely always thinking about what comes next.
