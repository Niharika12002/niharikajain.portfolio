This Portfolio is a Meta-Project

The site you're reading is also part of my work.

I built this portfolio the way I design everything — by directing, deciding, pushing back, and making it feel like me. AI was a collaborator. I was the creative director.

"AI proposes. I decide."

I use AI for:

scaffolding, layouts, generating starting points I can push back on

I direct:

every creative decision, visual system, tone, what stays and what goes

I push back on:

generic layouts, buzzwordy copy, anything that doesn't feel like Niharika

Let's make something
worth noticing.
