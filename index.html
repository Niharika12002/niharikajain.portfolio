Behind the Build

This portfolio is
also a case study.

"AI proposes. I decide."

I built this portfolio the way I design everything — directing, deciding, pushing back, and making it feel like me. AI was a tool I used with intention. Every prompt was art-directed. Every output critiqued.

The full record lives on GitHub: every version, every rejected direction, every moment I said "no, that's not it."

View on GitHub ↗
prompts.md
Prompting as Art Direction
Every AI prompt was a creative brief — written with intent, references, and a clear point of view about what I didn't want.
decisions.md
Direction, Not Generation
I made every visual and structural decision. AI generated starting points. I shaped, rejected, and refined until it felt like mine.
rejected-versions.txt
Record of Resistance
I documented every version that felt too generic or template-like. Knowing what doesn't feel like you is half the creative brief.
commits.log
Iteration in Public
Every decision is committed. The build history is a design process — public, honest, and ongoing.
