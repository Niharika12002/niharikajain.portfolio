about

Designer of calm systems, playful visuals, and tiny details.

I'm Niharika Jain, a UX designer with a graphic design background and a 2026 graduate of SCAD Atlanta. I'm drawn to messy systems, emotional products, physical-digital experiences, and visual details that make complex things easier to understand.

My work sits between structure and feeling: user flows, service systems, interface design, brand language, and the tiny decisions that make something easier to use, easier to trust, and more memorable.
