about

Designer of calm systems, playful visuals, and tiny details.

I'm Niharika Jain, a UX designer with a graphic design background, graduated from SCAD Atlanta in 2026. I'm drawn to messy systems, emotional products, physical-digital experiences, and visual details that make people stop for a second.

My work sits between structure and feeling: user flows, service systems, interface design, brand language, and the tiny decisions that make something easier to understand and easier to trust.
